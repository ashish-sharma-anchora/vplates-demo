    Restyle your number plates

    Keep your existing combination; change the design of your plate!

    Restyle my plate Create new plate

    Is your ride ready for a new look?

    Our Restyle option lets you change your existing number plate to a different design from as little as $260.

    Choose from a large range of colours and themes to find one that suits you and add that finishing touch to your car or motorbike.


    Restyle your plates in 3 easy steps!

    1. Enter your current plate combo.
    2. Choose your plate style and size.
    3. Confirm ownership to check out.

    Good to know

    • Restyle is only available for existing number plates registered in your name.
    • Designs you can Restyle into vary depending on your existing combination.
    • Restyling to a slimline sized plate is also available, with prices starting from $390.
    • Alternatively, if you want to create a brand new combination for a particular plate style, get started with our combination checker.
